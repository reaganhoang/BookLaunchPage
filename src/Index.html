The Miraculous Fountain of La Salette

It would have been fortunate if the tears that Mary shed at La Salette had fallen to the earth and been left on the mountain, as the bloody sweat of Jesus in the garden, to consecrate that place. But the children tell us that they fell and disappeared upon the crucifix over her heart, as the waters of a fountain returning to their source; a reminder that Mary's sorrow is unceasing and her tears celestial.

But she left a testimony in keeping with her tears, a fountain gushing from the heart of the mountain side. And, as unbelievers in the village below tested and ridiculed the little children as imposters, the spring gurgled in happy contradiction, the witness of a vision given by the power of God.
